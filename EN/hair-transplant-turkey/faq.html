Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is hair transplant?

A hair transplant is a surgical operation in which healthy and strong hair follicles are taken from the donor side of the patient to be transplanted into recipients areas of the head. Hair transplant may be performed to change the way the hair looks but also it helps to increase the self-confidence of patients.

Am I A Suitable Candidate For Hair Transplantation?

Although most people are suitable for hair transplant operation, some chronic diseases could prevent you from having this treatment. In addition, a patient needs to have a sufficient number of hairs on the donor area to conduct the treatment.

What should I do before a hair transplant?

  • Before the surgery you should inform the doctor about your medical health and usage of medicine.
  • With your doctor’s control you can discontinue to use blood-thinning medications such as aspirin at least 1 week before.
  • Since herbal teas such as green tea may increase bleeding, before surgery avoid foods and beverages that will cause bleeding. (Ginger, turmeric, garlic, red wine, some kinds of peppers, olive oil)
  • Since smoking can affect the healing process negatively, the patient should avoid consumption or use of tobacco and tobacco products.
  • 1 week before the hair transplant it is forbidden to consume alcohol or drink alcoholic products.

Why Memorial is the best place for hair transplantation

Due to our focus on patients satisfaction and treatment quality Memorial is considered the best location for hair transplantation in Turkey to receive hair transplant treatment.

Is hair transplant safe and permanent?

Hair transplantation is a unique and permanent solution to solve the loss of hair problem. Turkey is a world leading destination for patients who wish to undergo hair transplantation. Moreover, choosing an experienced team and center lead to the procedure is completely safe and successful. However, hair transplant patient candidates need to keep in their mind that, like all kinds of surgical operation, hair transplantation also has its own risks. In terms of comparing risks with other surgical operations, hair transplantation has a very low complication rate. General complications of hair transplantation can be pain after the operation, bleeding, edema, temporary or permanently shock hair loss.

Is Hair Transplant Painful?

In order to eliminate pain during the operation, local anesthesia is given to the patient in all hair transplant treatment. While local anesthesia is given by injection, patients feel the needle in its first contact to the head but the operation itself it's not painful.

Can the other patient's hair be transplanted as a donor?

There is no possibility to provide and use other patient’s follicles and hair. In terms of FUE hair transplantation, only your follicles can be transplanted.

How long after a hair transplant patient can do sport and exercise ?

First 3 weeks after the operation it is forbidden and not recommendable to do sport, swimming in the pool, solarium and sauna. After surgery, at Memorial Hair Transplantation Center, we provide oral and written documentation about the post-operative instructions.

Is there any age limitation for a hair transplant?

The hair transplant must be considered for adults and needs to be planned at the right time. If the patients are suitable for this operation and there is no obstacle such as health and previous medical conditions, hair transplant can be performed for individuals who are from 18 to 80 years old.

How do patients make an appointment for a hair transplant in Turkey?

The operation date can be organized according to patients’ availability and the availability of our doctor at the hair transplantation center. Once patients get the appointment, they can also book their flight to Istanbul.

How many days should patients stay in Turkey for a hair transplant?

Generally 3 days are sufficient for a hair transplant. It is obligatory that, once patients arrive in Turkey they have to do PCR tests in our hospitals. According to the PCR test results patients can undergo hair transplant. There is no need to stay at the hospital, because the duration of the operation is from 4 to 10 hours. After the hair transplant patients are going back to their hotel. On the second day of the operation there will be first washing and patients can go back to their countries.
