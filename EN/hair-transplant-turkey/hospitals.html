About

A World Hospital in the Center of Istanbul...

State-of-the-art technology, distinguished academic staff, patient-centered care understanding, the healing power of greenery and art, modern architectural design…

State-of-the-art technology, distinguished academic staff, patient-centered care understanding, the healing power of greenery and art, modern architectural design…

Being the 11th hospital project of Memorial Health Group, Memorial Bahcelievler serves to a total of 72 thousand square meters. There also is a green area of 8 thousand square meters. Equipped with the most advanced diagnostic and treatment units, the hospital holds the capacity of 320 beds, a total of 15 operating rooms, one of them being a hybrid operating room, 49 intensive care unit bed capacity, 135 outpatient clinics and 31 medical observation rooms.

Bahcelievler Hospital was built on the hospital care knowledge Memorial Health Group possesses. Being equipped with the cutting edge technology, superior staff who have achieved recognizance in national and international platforms, experienced and professional nursing staff who contribute to the physical and the psychological health of patients via their experience, conscious care and uplifted spirits Memorial Bahcelievler offers its services in a warm environment to patients and companions.

A Brand New Sense Of Health

In the design of the Memorial Bahçelievler Hospital, every detail was considered for the comfort of patients, their relatives as well as the employees. Starting from the fact that healing starts with the first step taken in the hospital; from the lobby area to the outpatient clinics, from patient waiting areas to physicians and patient rooms, everything was designed in such a way that it meets all the needs of the patients. Patients as well as their relatives have been provided the opportunity to comfortably pass their time as if they were in their living room, especially in the lounge areas located on the floor. From the choice of color, to the forms of the items, to the accessories selected without lighting, everything was planned to be a part of the healing process. Specialized diagnostic and treatment programs are offered with imaging units with superior technological features, complex laboratories, superior technology operating rooms and intensive care stations, hybrid operating rooms, robotic applications and specialized centers.

Specialized Clinics And Services

Memorial Bahcelievler Hospital offers its branch services divided into their own specialty fields regarding specialized treatment and surgeries.

Internationally Recognized Technological Infrastructure

Memorial Bahçelievler Hospital's technological infrastructure; has been equipped with one of the most advanced systems in cancer treatment, the new generation beam therapy Elekta Versa HD SIGNATURE, robotic surgery, radiological imaging systems (MR, CT, PET-CT, gamma camera, x-ray, mammography, fluoroscopy, ultrasound, echocardiography and angiography).

3 Tesla MR technology, one of the radiological imaging systems, stands out with its quality and reliable results. With high-tech dual energy CT, all parts of the body can be scanned in seconds. With Pet-CT technology, which has an important role in the diagnosis, staging and correct treatment of cancer, the patient is offered the possibility of scaning with the lowest dose of radioactive material. Da Vinci robotic surgery, which is particularly used in urology, kidney transplantation, gynecology, general surgery, allows a comfortable operation process for both patient and surgeon by taking a 3-dimensional image. bright intensive care units designed with a functional structure providing mobility for non-mobilized and unconscious patients are Turkey's first units that are using the Patient Lift technology as fixed in each room. In the areas where there are operating rooms designed with a functional structure and advanced technology, large living areas which were created by considering hygienic conditions for doctors and employees are noticed.

World’s First Leed Platinum Certified Full-Fledged Hospital

Memorial Bahcelievler Hospital has been awarded the most prestigious green building “LEED” Platinum” certificate which is given only to the leading institutions regarding energy and environment friendly design. Amongst the certificate programs of USGBC (United States Green Building Council) the certificate ranked to be the highest was given to a full-fledged hospital for the first time in the World and Turkey and that was Memorial Bahcelievler.

Green Hospital Concept

The interior and exterior spaces of the Memorial Bahçelievler Hospital, that has been built with an environmentally conscious approach, were designed by taking into consideration the positive effects of green spaces on human health and psychology. Living plants that will reflect all seasons, life cycles and biorhythms within the hospital, will make the patients and their relatives as well as the employees feel themselves present within the nature. The landscape arrangement, in which non-allergic natural plants have been preferred, also adds an aesthetic appeal to the architecture of the city. The Memorial Bahçelievler Hospital was built under consideration of environmental sensitivity, within a spectrum extending from energy efficiency to waste management and from sustainable area planning to indoor quality criteria.

The Healing Power Of Art

Memorial Bahçelievler Hospital draws attention with its "timeless designs" in which daylight is used to its maximum extend for the positive effects of green and the artistic healing power reflected in architecture on patients and their relatives' psychology. "Art" is one of the main themes of the Memorial Bahçelievler hospital that has been created by famous consultants on acoustics, landscape, lighting and design under the leadership of Memorial's experienced project team.

A Center Of Attraction Of Foreign Patients As Well

Memorial Bahçelievler, the 11th hospital of the Memorial Health Group, which accepts patients from 167 countries in the world, is also of great importance for foreign patients thanks to its specialized departments that are consisting a reference center in the international arena.

Memorial Bahçelievler Hospital Cardiology, Cardiovascular Surgery and Oncology departments serve under the agreement of SGK.
