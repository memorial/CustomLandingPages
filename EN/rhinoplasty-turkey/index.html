What is rhinoplasty?

Rhinoplasty is an operation which is performed to improve the breathing function of the nose or to better match with the eastetic requirements of the patient.

What are the benefits of rhinoplasty

  • Optimise the size of the nose
  • Give the nostrils a new shape
  • Reduce the nasal tip
  • Lowering Nose Bridge
  • Correct problems associated with a deviated septum
  • Improve breathing

What is the difference between open and closed rhinoplasty?

Open rhinoplasty is performed from outside of the nose, with a small incision placed on the columella. Closed rhinoplasty involves only incisions placed within the nostrils. The choice of procedure lies in which method will achieve optimal results.

How do I know if I'm a good candidate for rhinoplasty?

Rhinoplasty can be applied to everyone who is medically suitable to have a nose surgery.
Nasal aesthetics can be performed due to complaints such as the nose is arched, long, the tip of the nose is low, the size of the nostrils or asymmetry. A brief medical background and pictures of the nose will allow our doctors to confirm whether a treatment is possible.

What should I do before Rhinoplasty?

  • Before the surgery you should inform the doctor and anesthesiologist about your medical health and usage of medicine.
  • With your doctor’s control you can discontinue to use blood-thinning medications such as aspirin at least 1 week before.
  • If you are suffering from flu or cold symtoms you should share this with your doctor.
  • Since herbal teas such as green tea may increase bleeding, before surgery avoid foods and beverages that will cause bleeding. (Ginger, turmeric, garlic, red wine, some kinds of peppers, olive oil)
  • Since smoking can affect the healing process negatively, the patient should avoid smoking

Is Rhinoplasty Painful?

Pain can occur in every surgery. However, these pains can be controlled with painkillers.

Does rhinoplasty leave a scar?

Since the open technique used in nose aesthetic surgeries is performed by opening a small incision between the nostrils, there is a possibility of a slight trace of your nose in this part after nasal aesthetics. Since wound healing varies from person to person, this scar does not seem the same for everyone. In operations performed with closed technique, there is no scar visible from the outside.

How long is healing period for Rhinoplasty?

  • Nose plaster is used in the first 1 week, and nose bandage is used in the second week
  • the patient can Return to work after 1-2 weeks
  • The patient can conduct exercise after 2-3 weeks
  • Not to wear eyeglasses
  • Complete disappearance of swellings after 2 months
  • Final form of the nose after 1 year
