About The Department

Cancer has been the second most important but also life-threatening health problems in the world as well as in Turkey after the cardio-vascular diseases, and unfortunately the incidence is gradually increasing due to various reasons. Although there is a significant difference when considering each type of cancer, an individual's risk of developing a type of cancer is increasing day by day.

Cancer, thanks to the strategies developed in terms of diagnosis and treatment, is today among the manageable diseases. In particular, some types of cancer can ultimately be cured by eliminating risk factors, raising social awareness and early screening.

Today, the current achievement when considering the treatment of cancer, is the development of individual treatment strategies. Thanks to these methods determined according to the characteristics of the tumor, we can enhance the life quality of the patients. Nowadays, in addition to basic therapies against cancer such as surgery, chemotherapy and radiotherapy, a rather multidisciplinary approach is considered such as smart drugs targeted at the tumor cell together with standard chemotherapies, radiotherapy methods whose side effects are minimized, significant surgical methods, interventional radiology and special applications in the field of nuclear medicine. At the Memorial Chemotherapy Centers, a multidisciplinary approach including individual treatment programs, scientific approaches and technological infrastructure used by the specialist doctors, is preferred along with the classical therapies.

Chemotherapy which is considered as the golden standard of many types of cancers is still among the most important treatments of the disease. The chemotherapy applications shooting for to prevent the growth and spread of cancer cells are now planned specifically for patients and tumor cells. Smart drugs and antibody treatments targeting only defected tissues are successfully applied at Memorial Chemotherapy Centers, where scientific and current treatments are always concurrently utilized with the rest of the world.

The right choice of the drugs during chemotherapy is very important when considering both the success of the treatment and the minimization of patients' exposure to the side effects. Drug treatment in Memorial Chemotherapy Centers is programmed by successful medical onchology professionals with international experience, considering the type of tumor, age, general condition and other disease factors.
